Banana Pancakes

♪ ♫ Pretend like it's the weekend now ♫ ♪

I actually made these every week in quarantine and don't follow a recipe, oops

Just throw random stuff together until it has the right consistency, feel free to add more of whatever as ya please

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup oats
  • 1 ripe banana
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tbsp cinnamon
  • 1 tbsp ground flax seed
  • 1/3 cup chocolate chips
  • 1/3 cup peanut butter powder or protein powder
  • 2 tsp vanilla
  • 1 cup egg whites
  • 1 spray whipped cream
  • 1/3 cup mixed berries

Instructions

  1. Grind the oats in a blender until it is a fine flour texture
  2. Mash up the banana
  3. Mix together all the ingredients until smooth
  4. Heat a large non-stick skillet over a medium heat
  5. Pour pancake batter in the pan to form small-medium sized pancakes. Cook for two minutes or until small bubbles begin to form on the top. Flip and cook for another two minutes or until golden brown and set through.
  6. Serve with whipped cream and berriessss
