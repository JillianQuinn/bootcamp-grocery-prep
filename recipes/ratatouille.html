Ratatouille

Easy, tasty, and it has a wild name

Ingredients

  • 2 eggplants
  • 2 zucchinis
  • 2/3 jar marinara sauce
  • 1/4 cup parmesean or mozarella cheese
  • 1/2 bunch fresh basil
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Prehead oven to 375Â°F
  2. Grease pan with olive oil
  3. Slice zucchini and eggplant thin with a mandolin
  4. Put a layer of zucchini and eggplant slices on the bottom of the pan
  5. cover with marinara sauce, cheese, and basil
  6. continue layering until full
  7. top with more basil, cheese, and oregano
