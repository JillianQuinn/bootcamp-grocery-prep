Jorge's Chicken Soup Mexico City Style Copycat:

My fav mexican restaurant from when I lived in California and I missed it so this is my recreation

Ingredients

  • 1/2 diced onion
  • 2 diced medium tomatoes
  • 32 oz chicken broth
  • 4 chicken breasts boiled
  • 1 bunch chopped cilantro
  • 2 tsp Knorr tomato boullion with chicken flavor
  • 1 jalape√±o
  • 1 avocado

Instructions

  1. Heat a large pot on medium heat and add chicken broth
  2. Dice up ingredients and spices other than avocado and add to the pot
  3. Simmer until flavor develops
  4. Pour into bowls to serve and top with avocado slices and cilantro
