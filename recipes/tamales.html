Tamales

Quinn fam Christmas dinner

Ingredients

Dough

  • 2 cups Masa Harina Corn Flour
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1.5-2 cups warm water
  • 30 corn husks

Filling

  • 1 white onion
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1.5 cups shredded chicken or turkey
  • 1 poblano pepper
  • 2 habanero peppers
  • 4 jalepeño peppers
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 can sliced Black olives
  • 1 bunch Cilantro
  • Chili powder
  • Oregano
  • Cumin
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Paprika
  • Cayenne pepper
  • Chipotle seasoning

Instructions

  1. Add warm water to masa flower and salt slowly while mixing until well combined
  2. let dough sit covered for an hour
  3. Soak corn husks in warm water for 30 minutes until soft
  4. Cook onion in 2 tablespoons butter and ½ cup water until soft
  5. Add garlic, peppers, and spices to taste
  6. Let simmer for 3 minutes
  7. Mix in shredded meat and olives
  8. Remove from heat and mix in cilantro
  9. Lay a corn husk, glossy side up, on the counter with the wide end at the top
  10. Scoop about ¼ cup of dough onto the top, center of the corn husk
  11. Lay a piece of plastic wrap over the dough and use your hands to press and spread the masa into a thin layer, about 1/4 inch thick
  12. Place 1-2 tablespoons of filling in a line down the center of the dough
  13. Fold-in one long side of the husk over the filling, then fold the other long side, overlapping the first
  14. Fold the bottom of the husk up
  15. Tear a long strip from an edge of one of the soaked corn husks and use it to tie the tamale, to hold it together
  16. In an Instant Pot, cook on Manual/High Pressure for 25 minutes. Allow pressure to naturally release for 10 minutes, and then quick release.
