Midnight Chocolate Cake

My family's go to recipe for birthdays and bonus there's COFFEE IN THE CAKE

Number of Servings:

Serving Size: 9 in cake

1

Ingredients

  • 1 cup flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 0.5 cup unsweetened cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 0.5 cup milk
  • 1 tsp vanilla
  • 0.5 tsp baking powder
  • 0.25 tsp salt
  • 1 egg
  • 0.25 cup black coffee
  • 0.25 cup vegetable oil

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F
  2. Mix together flour, sugar, baking soda, baking powder, and salt
  3. Dissolve cocoa powder in hot black coffee
  4. Add milk to coffee mixture to cool down
  5. Mix liquid ingredients with dry ingredients until smooth
  6. Pour batter into buttered baking pan
  7. Bake at 350 degrees for 35 minutes

Bonus Recipe: Chocolate Frosting

I'd also recommend halving this recipe

  • 3 cups confectioners' sugar
  • 1 cup butter
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 tbsp whipping cream
  • (Optional) Meringue to make it thicc
  1. In a standing mixer fitted with a whisk, mix together sugar and butter.
  2. Mix on low speed until well blended
  3. Increase speed to medium and beat for another 3 minutes.
  4. Add vanilla and cream and continue to beat on medium speed for 1 minute more, adding more cream if needed for spreading consistency.
