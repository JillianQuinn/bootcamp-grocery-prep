Instant Pot Yogurt

Tastes almost as good as Ellenos and it's fun to make

It's like sourdough because you keep the yogurt starter going but it's less work than sourdough

Also this photo looks so ugly but its actually good

Number of Servings:

10

Ingredients

  • 52 ounces Ultra Pasteurized/Ultra-Filtered Milk
    • â€¢ if you use normal milk there's an extra boiling process :(
  • 2 tablespoons yogurt

Instructions

  1. Santize hand mixer beaters in the Instant Pot bowl with boiling water on the stove
  2. Mix together yogurt and milk with hand mixer in Instant Pot until well blended.
  3. Place lid on Instant Pot and select the yogurt button and "normal" setting
  4. Let incubate for 6-8 hours (the longer you go the more tangy it becomes)
  5. Open lid and remove inner pot
  6. Scoop/pour yogurt into a strainer and let sit in the fridge on the strainer overnight to make it Greek yogurt
  7. Once strained, discard strained whey and use the hand mixer to blend berries in with the yogurt
  8. Store in a glass container in the fridge for 10-14 days
