Zucchini Boats

Easy, tasty, and similar ingredients to ratatouille so meal prep is easy

Number of Servings:

1

Ingredients

  • 1 zucchini
  • 0.5 bell pepper
  • 0.5 cup marinara sauce
  • 0.25 cup parmesean cheese
  • 0.25 cup diced onion
  • 1 tsp minced garlic
  • 0.5 bunch fresh basil
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 0.5 tsp pepper
  • 1 tsp oregano
  • 3 oz shredded chicken or ground turkey

Instructions

  1. Prehead oven to 375Â°F
  2. Slice zucchini in half and scoop out middle
  3. Dice bell pepper and onion
  4. Mix together scooped out zucchini, marinara sauce, bell pepper, cheese, salt, garlic, onion, oregano, and meat
  5. Fill hollow zucchini with mixture
  6. Top with more cheese and basil
  7. Put in oven for 25 minutes or cheese is golden brown and zuchinni is tender or microwave for 12 minutes
